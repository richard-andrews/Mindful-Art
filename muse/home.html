How to participate:

  1. Carefully read the Participant Information Sheet

  2. If you are happy to participate and have no questions, sign the consent form

  3. Once to have received an ID number from the researcher, Richard Andrews, you may head to the survey page and complete the survey

  4. Next, enter you ID in the field below the survey and head to the page to begin experiencing Muse

  5. When you feel that you have explored Muse to the fullest extent (est. 5-10mins), please return to the survey page and complete the survey again

  6. You have then completed the study, thank you very much!


If you are unsure what to do, please feel free to refer to this page at any point by clicking the home tab at the top of the page.

If you wish to withdraw at any point or wish to ask a question, please contact either Richard or Daniela, their emails are at the bottom of every page.
